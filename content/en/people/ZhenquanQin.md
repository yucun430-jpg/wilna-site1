    content = "Zhenquan Qin, Ph.D., Associate Professor, Master's Supervisor, has obtained a bachelor's degree and a Ph.D. from University of Science and Technology of China in 2002 and 2007 respectively. He has led or participated in 8 national-level scientific research projects and has been awarded over 10 national invention patents and software copyrights. In recent years, he has mainly focused on research in the fields of wireless sensor networks, cognitive radio networks, and cross-technology communication, achieving a number of valuable research results. He has published over 60 papers in international conferences and journals such as SIGCOMM, INFOCOM, MOBISYS, SECON, MONET, and WCMC, with 21 papers indexed by SCI and over 50 indexed by EI; among them, 25 papers are first-authored and 16 are co-authored. The Remote Transmission Technology of Data via Wireless Ad Hoc Networks won the 2019 Liaoning Provincial Science and Technology Progress Award. Currently, his main research directions are:Trustworthy Federated Learning;Security Protocols Based on Wireless Sensing;Intelligent Data Mining for Industrial and Marine Internet of Things."
