    content = "Jiancheng Chi, Ph.D., Assistant Professor at the Software College and International Information and Software College of Dalian University of Technology, and a Master's Supervisor. He is a high-level talent in Dalian. He is a member of IEEE, ACM, and CCF, an executive member of the CCF Internet of Things Professional Committee, and serves as a reviewer for several top international journals such as IEEE TMC, and a chairperson and program committee member of academic conferences like SmartIoT and CNETCNED. He obtained a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from Dalian University of Technology in 2017, and Master's and Ph.D. degrees from Tianjin University in 2020 and 2025 respectively. His research focuses on industrial Internet of Things, industrial large models, edge computing and edge intelligence. His research results have been published in top journals such as IEEE TMC; he has authored one Springer Nature monograph, has 7 national invention patents and 3 international patents (from the United States and the Netherlands), and has over 1100 citations on Google Scholar. He has been awarded the ACM National Excellent Doctoral Dissertation Award (Dalian Branch), the CSCWD Best Paper Award, and other academic honors, as well as several awards such as the National Scholarship for Overseas Study and the Lenovo Scholarship of Tianjin University."
