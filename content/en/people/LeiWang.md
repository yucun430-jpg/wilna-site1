    content = "Lei Wang, Vice Dean of the Software College of Dalian University of Technology, Professor and Doctoral Supervisor, Deputy Director of the Key Laboratory of Pervasive Networking and Software Services in Liaoning Province, and Director of the Joint Laboratory of Dalian University of Technology - Zhanzi Dao Group. He obtained a bachelor's degree, master's degree, and doctoral degree in computer science from Tianjin University in 1995, 1998, and 2001 respectively. He was a postdoctoral fellow at Seoul National University (2006) and Washington State University in the United States (2007). He has worked at the Bell Laboratories China Research Institute (2001-2004) and Samsung Research Institute (South Korea) (2004-2006). He is a member of the expert working group of the Road Safety Technology Action Plan (12th Five-Year Plan) (Supporting Project) of the Ministry of Science and Technology. He is a member of the IEEE Computer Society and the IEEE Communications Society, an ACM Professional member, an advanced member of the Chinese Computer Society, a member of the Wireless Sensor Network Special Committee, and the Executive Vice Chairman of the ACM Dalian Chapter. His research fields include Internet of Things, wireless networks, and network security. He has led 2 national natural science foundation projects of the Ministry of Science and Technology, 1 natural science foundation project of Liaoning Province, and 1 horizontal project such as the Nokia Global Fund, as well as a sub-project of the key research and development plan of the Ministry of Science and Technology. He has published over 90 papers, including high-level papers in INFOCOM and other journals, with a total citation count exceeding 1700 times."
